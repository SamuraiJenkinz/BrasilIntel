Cron Expression Reference

Cron expressions follow the format: minute hour day-of-month month day-of-week

Expression Meaning
0 6 * * * Daily at 6:00 AM
0 7 * * 1-5 Weekdays at 7:00 AM
0 8 * * 1 Mondays at 8:00 AM
30 9 * * * Daily at 9:30 AM
0 */2 * * * Every 2 hours
Note: Modify schedules via environment variables:
SCHEDULE_HEALTH_CRON, SCHEDULE_DENTAL_CRON, SCHEDULE_GROUP_LIFE_CRON
All times are in Sao Paulo timezone (America/Sao_Paulo).
